A Little Life


Author name: Hanya Yanagihara

Description: A Little Life follows four college classmates- broke, adrift and buoyed only by thier friendship and ambition- as they move to New York in search of fame and fortune. While thier relationships, which are tinged by addiction, success and pride, deepen over the decades, the men are held together by thier devotion to the brilliant, enigmatic Jude, a man scarred by an unspeakable childhood trauma.
